About Me

As a passionate SciFi fan and gamer, I have always been interested in the latest technical developments. In early 2022 I decided to start my web development journey with a few courses and small projects. Since I quickly realized that I enjoy web programming, I decided to attend a professional training course to become a web developer in Germany. I developed irrepressible motivation there and made progress very quickly. Creating realistic projects daily as well as getting code reviews from experienced developers contributed to my fast learning, so I developed a lot of front-end applications in the last few months. I'm looking forward to building on my experience and starting my career in IT in a junior developer position, developing the really cool stuff.
